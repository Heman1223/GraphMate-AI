Friend Recommendation System

Discover and connect with new friends using advanced graph algorithms and shared interest analysis.

Data Structure Used

Adjacency list for the social graph with hash sets for quick interest lookups.

Algorithm

BFS explores up to 2 degrees of separation and calculates mutual interests.

Time Complexity

O(V + E) for traversal plus O(I) for interest matching.

Scalability

Optimized for large networks with efficient space and time performance.

Real-world Use

Similar concepts power friend suggestions on major social platforms.

Why It Works

Combines graph theory and interest similarity to suggest relevant friends.
